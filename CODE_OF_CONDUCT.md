# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

We are committed to providing a welcoming and professional environment. Examples of expected behavior:

- Being respectful and constructive in discussions
- Focusing on what is best for the project and its users
- Accepting constructive criticism gracefully

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to all project spaces including issues, pull requests, and discussions.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainers. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated.
