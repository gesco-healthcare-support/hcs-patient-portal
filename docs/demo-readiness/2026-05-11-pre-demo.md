## Live-demo recipe (after fixes applied)

1. **Open ALL 4 external user sessions in SEPARATE browser profiles or
   incognito tabs.** Avoids the Bug D session-replace corner case (each
   profile keeps an independent SPA storage; no swap can occur).
2. **Use SoftwareThree, SoftwareFive, SoftwareSix as the visible
   recipients.** Their inbox routes received Requested + Approved +
   Packet attachment emails correctly in the verification run. Use
   SoftwareFour as a "we also CC'd this user" mention but do not open
   their inbox during demo — emails route to Junk on that specific
   mailbox.
3. **Show the packet attachment in SoftwareThree's inbox** —
   `A00009_Patient Packet_*.pdf` (449 KB) verifies end-to-end.
4. **Show packet download from the UI** — click any Download button on
   the Appointment Packets section of an Approved appointment. The PDF
   downloads via the browser's authenticated path.
5. **Show CE notification** — book as Three; show that Six receives an
   `AppointmentRequested/ClaimExaminer` email in their inbox. Pre-fix
   this never fired.
6. **Lead-time enforcement** — pick "today" in the booking datepicker;
   it's disabled (3-day minimum lead time, enforced by Angular). Out-
   of-window booking is rejected client-side.
7. **Approve / Reject flow** — both work end-to-end. Approve auto-fires
   packet generation; Reject does not. Stakeholder fan-out includes all
   four external roles for both.
8. **Invite external user** — the dev-mode banner is honest about
   sandbox-only delivery; show the Copy Link button + paste manually
   for the demo.
